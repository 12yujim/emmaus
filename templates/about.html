About

Emmaus Road is the English Ministry of KCC, the Korean Church at Cornell. Emmaus Road was called KCCE (Korean Church at Cornell English) until summer 2013.

The name “Emmaus Road” refers to story in Luke 24:13-35, where Jesus appears to two disciples on the road to Emmaus after His resurrection. The logo is inspired by Luke 24:32: “Did not our hearts burn within us while he talked to us on the road, while he opened to us the Scriptures?”

Vision

Emmaus Road exists to spread a passion for the supremacy of God in all things through Jesus Christ. Our core values are Gospel-centered, Christ-exalting, and Bible-saturated. You can read more about our values here.
